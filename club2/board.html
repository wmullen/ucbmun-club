Officers Board

  • Adam B. Spaulding

    Secretary-General

    Adam B. Spaulding

    Adam Spaulding survived 12 different schools in 16 years of schooling to call himself a senior this year. He grew up a military brat, the contributing factor to his love of History and Political Science as his parents dragged him (kicking and screaming) to political, historical, and geographic points of interest around the world. For Adam listening to pop-punk, socializing with his fraternity brothers, and applying to law schools constitute a good time. His involvement in Model UN started with a one-year stint on the European High School circuit and has since continued on the travel team here at Berkeley, but he still can’t decide if annoying crisis directors or creating a bloc as Libya or North Korea is his favorite aspect of Model UN. Most importantly, Adam cannot wait to see friends, meet new people, and provide a one-of-a-kind experience that he knows UCBMUN XVIII will be.

    Email: sg@ucbmun.org

    Deputy Secretary-General

    Anirudh Garg

    Anirudh Garg, hailing from the princely city of New Delhi, is a fourth year studying Electrical Engineering and Computer Science. He has been a part of Model UN for five years and has a deep passion for the mutually systematic and harsh mechanisms of South Asian politics. His interests, outside of MUN, lie in politically-incorrect humor, Batman, driving, and the philosophical works by Randall Munroe, Kris Wilson, Rob DenBleyker, Matt Melvin, and Dave McElfatrick.
